How to make pasta

Making pasta is one of the building blocks of learning how to cook. It's not difficult, but should be done correctly to complement the many delicious dishes that use pasta as a base.

Ingredients

  • Large pot
  • Strainer
  • Water for boiling
  • Salt
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Pour water into the large pot and add salt.
  2. Boil the water.
  3. Add the pasta to the boiling water
  4. Cook for six to eight minutes based on preference. Most people prefer al dente.
  5. Pour the water/pasta into the sink through the strainer to drain out the water.
  6. Plate the cooked pasta and add the desired sauces and toppings.
